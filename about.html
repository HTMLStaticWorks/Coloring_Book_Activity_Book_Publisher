About ColorSpark

A team of passionate artists, educators, and parents on a mission to spark creativity in every child, teen, and adult through the magic of coloring and creative activity.

How It All Started

The ColorSpark Story

ColorSpark was born in 2018 from a simple observation: coloring books were either too generic, too childish, or simply not educational enough. Our founders — a mother and a teacher — set out to change that.

Starting with a single animal coloring book printed at a local shop and sold at a school fair, ColorSpark quickly grew word-of-mouth into a beloved brand trusted by families across the globe. What began as a passion project now serves over 50,000 families and 1,200 schools.

Every ColorSpark book is crafted with love, peer-reviewed by educators, and designed to make learning feel like an adventure. We believe that when creativity and education meet, something magical happens.

Behind the Scenes

How We Create a ColorSpark Book

From concept to your child's hands — every book goes through a 5-step creative and educational review process.

1
Theme Research
Our team researches educational themes, age-appropriate content, and current curriculum standards before a single sketch is drawn.
2
Concept Design
Illustrators create initial character and scene concepts, balancing artistic appeal with the right level of complexity for the target age group.
3
Educator Review
Every book is reviewed by certified educators and child development specialists who ensure the content is age-appropriate and educationally rich.
4
Illustration & Inking
Final illustrations are created digitally with crisp, clean lines optimized for both screen display and high-quality printing.
5
Child Testing
Real kids test every book! We observe which pages engage them most and make refinements based on genuine child feedback.
"We believe creativity is not a talent — it's a skill that every child deserves the opportunity to develop. When we put a blank coloring page in front of a child, we're not just asking them to stay in the lines. We're inviting them to see the world in color."
— Sarah Chen, Co-founder of ColorSpark

Our Journey

ColorSpark Timeline

2018
The First Book
Sarah and Marcus publish their first hand-crafted Animal Kingdom coloring book at a school fair. 200 copies sell out in one afternoon.
2019
Going Digital
ColorSpark launches its online store, offering 12 PDF download titles. Monthly sales reach 1,000 within 6 months.
2020
Supporting Families During Lockdown
During the pandemic, ColorSpark offers 50 free coloring pages and gains an audience of 100,000 families across social media.
2021
First School Partnership
Oakwood Primary School becomes our first bulk-order partner. The school program launches with 50 partner schools by year-end.
2023
500 Books Milestone
ColorSpark publishes its 500th title, covering 9 major themed collections. The team grows to 18 full-time staff members.
2026
Serving 50,000+ Families
ColorSpark now serves over 50,000 families and 1,200 schools worldwide, with new books launching every month.
