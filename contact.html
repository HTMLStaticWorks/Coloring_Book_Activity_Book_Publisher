Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about digital downloads, printing, shipping, and school orders.

How do I download my digital purchase?
+
After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with a download link within 5 minutes. You can also access all your purchases in your account under "My Downloads."
What paper size should I use for printing?
+
Our PDFs are formatted for both US Letter (8.5"x11") and A4 pages. Make sure to set your printer settings to "Fit to Page" for the best results. We recommend heavy weight paper or cardstock for coloring.
How long does physical book shipping take?
+
Standard US shipping takes 5–7 business days, and expedited shipping takes 2–3 days. International shipping can take 10–21 business days depending on customs.
Do you offer school or bulk order discounts?
+
Yes! We offer bulk discounts starting at 20% off for 10+ books, 35% off for 25+ books, and up to 50% off for 100+ books. We also process official Purchase Orders.
What is your returns and refund policy?
+
Physical books can be returned within 30 days of delivery if unused for a full refund. Digital purchases are non-refundable once downloaded, but you can preview sample pages for free beforehand.
